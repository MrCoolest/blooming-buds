Admissions & Eligibility

Prashant Junior College is Affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education. The admissions to Standard XI are scheduled around the date the Maharashtra State Board declares its SSC results. The admission process is centralized by the Government and is monitored and coordinated by the Office of the Deputy Director of Education, Greater Mumbai. The whole process of admission is online, and students have to fill in the admission form giving preference of colleges. Based on the aggregate marks, the category and the preference of colleges as selected by the candidates, the allotment of seats for admission are announced by the online system.
