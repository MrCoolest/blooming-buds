Trustee Sir

            Teacher with a great vision and a well-known personality. He believes that every Bloomites is to be educated moulded each buds bloom in its own true colours. Whatever he does he believes in giving his best shot.

I started Hindi School in 1993 for all the under privileged children. My dream for an English Medium School & College with quality education was accomplished in 2010 and started Blooming Buds High School and Prashant Junior College in 2018 -2019, Vithalwadi (West), Ulhasnagar.

We are motivated enough to give the best exposure to our learners and inculcating hand on experience in the current education scenario which is the need of the hour and wish to transform the life of every single learner at Prashant Junior College to make educated, responsible, ethical citizens and value based global leaders of tomorrow. Joyful years of Blooming and learning in a stimulating environment.
