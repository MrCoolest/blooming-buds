  Beyond Academics    

Co-Curricular

  • Intra class competitions are held in Indoor Games, Quiz, Drawing, Essay, Handwriting, Dance and Singing to recognize their inherent talents and to develop competitive spirit.
  • “Vibration” an international program is conducted twice a year for all the classes. A parent handles the classroom and facilitates the students learning.
  • Open day is scheduled after examination to demonstrate the progress of the student.
  • PTA Meetings are held twice in a year for discussion and sharing information based on educational issues.
  • Remedial classes are conducted for weaker students to enhance their learning skills.

Sports

  • We have sports activities for all age groups so as everyone can enjoy, relax and exercise at the same time.
  • We, here at Blooming Buds, want the students to find themselves, and the only way we can let them find themselves is by letting them play and live life to the fullest. That is our eternal aim-to let the student LIVE LIFE TO THE MAXIMUM.
  • Our sports activates include but are not limited to: carom, Chess, Ludo, Table Tennis, Basket Ball, Football, Kho-Kho, Cricket etc.
