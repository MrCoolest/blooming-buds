  Academics    

Pre-primary/ Primary / Secondary

  • Admissions are accepted throughout the year from the age of 2.5yrs.
  • Classrooms are airy, spacious & well equipped with audio visual aid.
  • Pre-primary curriculum follows the philosophy of whole brain teaching through development approach and it is well inter linked to make the journey smooth till STD I.
  • From primary school, every child is formatively assessed through oral skills, projects, worksheets, notebook completion, class test, unit test.
  • At the end of each term, child is summatively assessed which is based on oral and written test.
  • Teaching staff is well qualified, trained and computer savvy.
  • Professional training is provided throughout the year to develop skills among the teachers.
  • A fully child centric and progressive curriculum which blends scholastic and co-scholastic areas to enhance overall development of student.
  • Associated with non-profit organization which uplifts the overall school performance to the international standards.
  • Worksheets are provided for each unit to make learning more interesting and meaningful.
  • Eco-friendly workshops are arranged in each term to empower education through environment.
  • Festivals are celebrated throughout the year to fervor the feeling of brotherhood, unity and equality
  • Report card is quite comprehensive which depicts performance based on academics and behaviors for each term.
